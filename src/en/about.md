<h1>About Teng-Ko Weng</h1>

<p>
  Teng-Ko Weng (翁登科) is a Taiwan-born artist based in Burnaby, British Columbia, Canada.
  His work includes oil painting, abstract experimentation, and years of teaching practice.
</p>

<h2 class="section-title">Artist Statement</h2>
<p>
  Artistic creation is a language of life. Through texture, color, and rhythm, each piece reflects
  memory, hope, and gratitude. Faith has shaped both his life and long artistic journey.
</p>

<h2 class="section-title">Background</h2>
<ul class="list-clean">
  <li>Born in Taipei, Taiwan</li>
  <li>Immigrated to Canada and now lives in Burnaby, BC</li>
  <li>Professional artist and senior member of FCA (SFCA)</li>
  <li>Long-term art educator with decades of teaching experience</li>
</ul>
